About Us - Down the River Adventures

At Down the River Adventures, our purpose is to help every explorer—young or old—discover the joy, courage, and connection that come from navigating wild waters. Our mission is to create safe, unforgettable river journeys where families, friends, and solo adventurers can leave the noise behind and find something real.

History

Down the River Adventures began as a small family-owned rafting company along the Guadalupe River.

What started as weekend runs grew into a full outdoor experience serving thrill-seekers from across the country.
